
# UniPaith Landing Page

## Brand Identity
- **Color palette**: Warm, approachable tones — primary deep teal (#0D7377), secondary warm coral (#F4845F), accent golden yellow (#F7D154), backgrounds in soft cream (#FFF8F0) and light gray
- **Typography**: Rounded, friendly headings (system fonts with soft weight), clean body text
- **Style**: Rounded corners, soft shadows, friendly illustrations/icons, plenty of whitespace

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section
- Tagline: *"Apply once, go anywhere"* with subtitle explaining UniPaith connects students and institutions through AI
- Two CTAs side by side: **"Create Free Account"** (students) and **"Request a Demo"** (institutions)
- Abstract illustration or gradient background with floating UI mockup elements

### 2. Problem Statement
- Split visual: Student side ("Overwhelmed by applications?") vs. Institution side ("Drowning in admin?") — showing the pain points UniPaith solves

### 3. How It Works — Students
- 3-step visual flow: **Build Your Universal Profile** → **Discover & Match Programs** → **Apply & Track Everything**
- Emphasis on AI-guided discovery, readiness tracking, and one-profile-fits-all

### 4. How It Works — Institutions
- Feature cards: **Standardized Intake**, **AI-Powered Review Tools**, **Integrity & Compliance**, **Data-Driven Insights**
- Focus on reduced admin burden, better applicant matching, audit-ready workflows

### 5. AI Features Showcase
- Visual section highlighting key AI capabilities: smart matching, document parsing, readiness diagnostics, anomaly detection
- Warm, approachable icons with brief descriptions

### 6. The Flywheel / Network Effect
- Simple diagram showing how more participation → better matching → better outcomes for everyone

### 7. Social Proof / Trust
- Placeholder for testimonials, partner logos, stats (e.g., "X applications simplified")

### 8. Dual CTA Section
- Students: "Start your journey — it's free" → Sign up button
- Institutions: "See how UniPaith transforms admissions" → Request Demo button

### 9. Footer
- Links, contact info, tagline reminder

## Responsive Design
- Fully responsive with mobile-first considerations
- Hamburger nav on mobile, stacked sections
